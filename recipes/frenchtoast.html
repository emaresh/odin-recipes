Very Vanilla French Toast Recipe

Very Vanilla French Toast

These French toast slices have vanilla flavor from convenient pudding mix, plus hint of cinnamon.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 2 tsp. butter
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 pkg. 3 oz "Cook and Serve" vanilla pudding mix
  • slices of Texas toast bread

    • Steps

    1. In large bowl, whish milk, pudding mix, egg, and cinnamon for 2 minutes.
    2. In large cast iron or other heavy skillet, melt butter over medium heat.
    3. Dip toast in pudding mixture, coating both sides.
    4. Cook the bread on both sides until golden brown.
