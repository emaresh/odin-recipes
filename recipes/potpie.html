Chicken Pot Pie Recipe

Chicken Pot Pie

This Classic Chicken Pot Pie has a flaky, buttery crust, a creamy sauce, and a hearty mix of chicken and vegetables your family will love.

Ingredients

  • 2 cans cream of chicken soup
  • frozen vegetables
  • 1 can of potatoes
  • 1 can of milk
  • grilled chicken
  • pie crust
  • pepper, garlic & onion powder, salt, tyme, rosemary

    • Steps

    1. season and grill the chicken
    2. grab a large mixing bowl
    3. mix together all of the ingredients
    4. preheat oven to 425
    5. put pie crust in pie dish
    6. add mixed ingredents into pie crust
    7. top the pic crust and ingredients with another pie crust
    8. cut 4 slits on the top of the pie crust
    9. place pot pie into oven for 35 minutes
