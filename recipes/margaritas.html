Best Margarita Ever

This is my take on a classic margarita with the perfect ratio of ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 2oz of blanco tequila (Casamigos preferred)
  • 1oz fresh squeezed lime juice
  • 1oz orange liquor (Cointreua preferred)
  • 1/4oz agave nectar

    • Steps

    1. Grab a cocktail shaker.
    2. Mix together tequila, lime juice, orange liquor and agave nectar.
    3. Add ice and seal the lid on the shaker.
    4. Prepare glass with salt (if wanted), and add ice.
    5. Shake aggressivly for 30 seconds or so.
    6. Poor over ice.
    7. Add lime wheel to glass for garnish.
    8. Sip and enjoy.
